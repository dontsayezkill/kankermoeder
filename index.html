Treasures

The monkeys have treasures on board. If you manage to capture the monkeys' ship without sinking the ship, you might be able to get your hands on them!

Currency

After collecting enough bananacoins from stolen treasures, you can buy chests at the treasure chest shop. These chests contain weapon upgrades for your ship, with varying rarity. You can use the upgrades or sell them to other players.
